Hidden

As official consort to Magister Dorian Pavus, Kai Trevelyan is now a permanent resident of Tevinter. But like so many things in the ancient Imperium, that happiness comes with a price. And as Kai and Dorian discover, whether it be in Minrathous, in unexplored catacombs, or in their own home in Qarinus, beneath the glittering surface, dark and secret things are hidden.
